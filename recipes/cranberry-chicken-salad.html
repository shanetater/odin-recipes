Cranberry Chicken Salad

Description

This cranberry chicken salad with tender chopped chicken, tart cranberries, apple, and crunchy pecans is delicious on lettuce or in a sandwich or wrap. A great way to use up leftover chicken!

Ingredients

  • 2 cooked chicken breast halves, chopped
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sweetened dried cranberries
  • 1/4 green apple, shredded
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Stir chicken and mayonnaise together in a medium bowl until evenly coated. Stir in green onions, dried cranberries, apple, pecans, lime juice, dill weed, salt, and pepper.
  2. Serve immediately or refrigerate for several hours or overnight (the flavor just gets better).
